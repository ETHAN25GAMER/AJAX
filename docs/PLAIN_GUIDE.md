# PestLLM — A Simple Guide

> A plain-language explanation of what this system is and what it does — no
> technical background needed. If you run the business or work in it, this is
> the one to read.

---

## In one sentence

PestLLM is a smart assistant that lives on your company's WhatsApp number. It
chats with customers day and night — answering questions, giving prices, booking
appointments, and knowing when to hand things to a real person — so your team
doesn't have to reply to every message by hand.

The assistant introduces itself to customers as **"Ajax"**.

---

## What the customer sees

A customer sends a WhatsApp message to your business number, just like texting a
friend. Ajax replies in seconds, any time of day. In a normal conversation it can:

- **Answer questions** about your services in a friendly, natural way.
- **Give a price** — always from your real price list, never a made-up number.
- **Identify a pest from a photo** — the customer snaps a picture of the bug, and
  Ajax tells them what it likely is and what service they need.
- **Book an appointment** — it checks what times are free, offers a slot, and
  confirms the booking with a short code (like a ticket number).
- **Reschedule or cancel** — using that same code.
- **Handle yearly plans** — if a customer wants an annual maintenance contract or
  wants to renew one, Ajax takes the request and passes it to your team.
- **Know its limits** — if someone was bitten or stung, has a complaint, or needs
  something unusual, Ajax immediately alerts a real person instead of guessing.

The whole thing feels like chatting with a helpful, well-trained employee who
never sleeps and never forgets your prices.

---

## What your office team sees

Managers and owners log in to a **web dashboard** from any computer or phone —
no app to install. From there they can:

| Section | What it's for |
|---|---|
| **Appointments** | All upcoming jobs, laid out by day. |
| **Dispatch** | A live map showing where each technician is right now. |
| **Conversations** | The full chat history with any customer. |
| **Escalations** | The list of chats Ajax flagged for a human to handle. |
| **Pricing** | Your price list — change a price and Ajax uses the new one instantly. |
| **Contracts (AMC)** | All your annual maintenance plans and their renewal dates. |
| **Reports** | Revenue, job counts, and how each technician is doing. |
| **Team** | Add or remove staff and set who can see what. |

Everything updates live. If one manager assigns a job, everyone else sees it
change on their screen straight away.

---

## What your technicians see

Technicians get a **mobile app** on their phone (it installs from the web — no app
store needed). It shows only their own jobs. On it they can:

- See today's jobs with the customer's name, address, and phone number.
- Tap the address to open Google Maps, or tap the number to call.
- Press **"Start travel"** — this shares their live location so the customer gets a
  tracking link, just like a food-delivery app.
- Add notes and upload before/after photos from the job.
- Mark a job as finished.
- Flag a problem for the office.

---

## Messages that go out on their own

Ajax also sends helpful messages automatically, so nobody has to remember to:

- **Remind** customers the day before their appointment.
- **Nudge** a customer who started chatting but went quiet.
- **Alert** a customer when the technician is on the way (with the tracking link).
- **Remind** customers when their yearly plan is about to expire.

If a customer ever replies **STOP**, Ajax immediately stops the marketing-style
messages — but still sends the important ones like booking confirmations and
"technician on the way" alerts, because those are part of the service they booked.

---

## Privacy and peace of mind

- Old chat history is **deleted automatically after six months**.
- Customers can opt out of promotional messages any time by replying STOP.
- The system follows India's data-protection rules and has a privacy notice
  customers can read.

---

## Why it helps the business

- **Replies instantly, 24/7** — no missed messages, no waiting until morning.
- **Never quotes the wrong price** — it only uses the price list you control.
- **Books jobs while you sleep** — appointments land in the dashboard automatically.
- **Frees up your staff** — they handle the tricky cases; Ajax handles the routine ones.
- **Keeps customers informed** — reminders and live tracking mean fewer no-shows
  and fewer "where's the technician?" calls.

---

## What it doesn't do (just so it's clear)

- It doesn't take payments or deposits — your team handles money directly.
- It doesn't understand voice notes yet — customers should type or send photos.
- It won't pretend to handle an emergency — anything urgent goes straight to a human.

---

*In short: Ajax is a tireless front-desk assistant on WhatsApp, backed by a
dashboard for your office and an app for your field team.*
